🎛️ Beat Matching Cheat Codes

Auto-Phrasing

Count 32 beats, mix on the 33rd. Every transition sounds natural.

1... 2... 3... 4... 32... MIX! → Seamless

The Camelot Wheel

Stay in key: 1A→2A→3A (or adjacent). Harmonic mixing, always sounds good.

The 1-2 Punch

Drop on the "1" of a 4-bar phrase. Crowd loses it every time.

The Sneaky Blend

Kill lows on incoming track, swap bass at the drop. Invisible transition.

The Hard Cut

Stop track A dead, drop B on beat. Works at 128+ BPM. Guaranteed energy.
